Just Mine It

Just Mine It, is a game that you are suppose to mine! "Well no shit there Sherlock!" It is not a ripoff game from Minecraft! Think this sidebar as a TODO list, that "me" the creator would like to add before making into the beta stage!

In the year where humans finally decides to take-off, and further invest into space exploration, due to lack of resources that Earth has to provide. Our astronuts, will need to explore intergalatic planets in order to collect resources.

Instruction

  • Press WASD key to move your character.
  • Press T to explode with a bomb!
  • Press B to buy bombs!
